Crystal Collector

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score dependent upon the amount of points each crystal is worth within each game.
You win by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is unkown to you until you click on it.
